Metallic Sound


High-Frequency Noise

It is hard for our vocoder to track high-frequency components. If our model synthesizes inaccurate harmonic elements and a series of noises, the result will just be metallic.

A closer look

As illustrated below, we use a high pass filter to get the high-frequency parts of two audio samples. The first is the ground truth, and the second is the prediction.

Aperiodic Transitions.

Aperiodic transitions are also hard for neural vocoders to learn, thus metallic sounds will also occur in those places.
