Overview

In this part, you will learn some general artifacts in audio generation, including the aliasing problem, the periodicity mismatch problem, etc. These problems are easy to understand and identify in an audio file. You will also learn some advanced artifacts, including the mouth click distortion, phase distortion, etc.

When you finish browsing this tutorial, press "Finished" to start the evaluation.
