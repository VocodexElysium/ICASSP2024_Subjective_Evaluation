Mouth Click Distortion


What is a mouth click?

Mouth-clicks sounds like little clicks or pops that are during, before or after voicing. Almost all human beings have some kind of mouth noise when they talk or sing. It's caused by the tongue, teeth and saliva creating little bubbles, clicks and pops as the voice is made.

What is mouth click distortion?

Mouth clicks usually occur as aperiodic parts in the singing voice, making it hard for our vocoder to resynthesis. Without enough attention to the details in the frequency domain, the click will be resynthesized into noises.
